Our Story

Bold Flavours,
Born in Kuje

Burger Town isn't just another fast-food spot — it's a movement. We were founded with one mission: to bring world-class crispy chicken, loaded burgers, and creamy shakes to the heart of Kuje, Abuja, at prices that make sense.

Every piece of chicken is hand-battered with our secret 12-spice blend, every burger is smashed on a searing hot griddle, and every shake is blended thick enough to stand a straw in. We're not cutting corners — we're cutting patties.
